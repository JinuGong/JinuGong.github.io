About

I am a faculty member in the Department of Applied Artificial Intelligence at Hansung University, where I lead the TAC Lab — Trustworthy AI and 6G Communications. My research sits at the intersection of trustworthy machine learning and next-generation wireless communications, aiming to make AI systems both statistically principled and practically deployable.

Research Interests

  • Machine Learning: machine unlearning, Bayesian learning, federated learning, Kolmogorov–Arnold networks (KANs), explainable AI (XAI)
  • Wireless Communications: channel estimation and prediction, performance analysis over fading channels, secure communications
